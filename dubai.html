Dubai in the Past

Once a modest coastal settlement, Dubai’s foundation was built on pearl diving, fishing, and regional trade. Community, resilience, and ambition shaped the city’s early identity.

  • Pearl diving and maritime trade

Dubai • 2025

From Desert to Global Icon

In just a few decades, Dubai transformed into one of the world’s most influential cities — a global destination for business, luxury living, and real estate investment.

  • World-class infrastructure and iconic architecture
  • Global business and financial hub
  • One of the strongest real estate markets worldwide
